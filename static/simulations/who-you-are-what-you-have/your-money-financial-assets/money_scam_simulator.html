Greed & Scarcity

Pig Butchering Scam

🔍 The Scam

Attackers spend weeks building a trusting friendship or romance, then pitch a 'guaranteed returns' crypto or forex platform that is entirely fake.

💬 What It Sounds Like
"Rohan, I've been using this automated trading bot for 6 months, and I consistently make 3% weekly returns. Let me show you how to get started—just deposit $500 and watch it grow."
🛡️ Defense Checklist
  • Never invest based on an online friend's advice without independent verification.
  • Test any platform with a tiny amount and attempt to withdraw before committing more.
