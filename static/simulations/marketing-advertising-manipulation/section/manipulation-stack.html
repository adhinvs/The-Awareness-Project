Layer 1 - Bottom

Data Collection

⌄

Every click, search, purchase, location check-in, and app interaction is logged. Tracking pixels, cookies, device fingerprints, and app SDKs feed this data to exchanges and brokers.

You are generating this data whether or not you are aware of it, logged in or not.

Layer 2

Profile Construction

⌄

Data brokers aggregate signals from hundreds of sources into a single file. They assign segment labels: "financially distressed," "health-anxious," "new parent," "politically persuadable."

These segments are available for purchase. They describe you without your knowledge or consent.

Layer 3

Targeting Engine

⌄

Advertisers use lookalike audiences, vulnerability scoring, and emotional state detection to decide exactly who to reach and when. Political campaigns use this layer for microtargeting.

The targeting engine is where your profile is matched to a commercial or political objective.

Layer 4

Message Optimisation

⌄

A/B tested copy selects the emotional lever most likely to work on your segment - fear, desire, identity, or shame. Thousands of ad variations are tested and the highest-converting version is served.

The message you see has already beaten thousands of alternatives in a conversion test.

The Ad

⌄

A simple, helpful-looking recommendation appears in your feed. It seems relevant to your interests. It appears at a moment when you are likely to be receptive.

This is the output of a five-layer commercial pipeline you never see. The ad is not the start of the process. It is the end.
