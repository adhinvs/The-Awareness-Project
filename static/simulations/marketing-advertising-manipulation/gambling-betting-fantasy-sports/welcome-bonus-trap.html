To access the "free" Rs 5,000, you must bet Rs 1,00,000 in total. At standard house edge, you will statistically lose between Rs 3,000 and Rs 8,000 in the process. The bonus does not give you money. It gives you a reason to deposit.

How to evaluate any welcome bonus Total wagering required = (deposit + bonus) x wagering multiplier. If that number is larger than you would comfortably lose, the bonus has no real value for you. The offer exists to create your first deposit - not to reward you for making it.
