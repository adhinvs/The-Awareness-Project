    How this works

    Photos stored on your phone often include an invisible EXIF data packet. That packet can contain:

    • GPS coordinates
    • camera make and model
    • date and time taken
    • orientation, shutter speed, and more

    When you export the image from a canvas, the resulting file is stripped of this metadata.
