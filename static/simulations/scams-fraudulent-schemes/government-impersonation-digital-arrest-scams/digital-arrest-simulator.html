Digital Arrest Call Simulator

You receive a call from someone claiming to be from the CBI Cyber Wing. Your Aadhaar is linked to a money laundering case. Work through each moment to see what happens.

Step 1 of 5

What Every Choice Revealed

  • Hanging up immediately is always the right first move. A real investigation survives a call ending.
  • "Digital arrest" has no legal basis. No Indian law permits detention over a call or video.
  • Personal data proves nothing. Attackers buy Aadhaar and PAN data from breached databases.
  • Isolation is the weapon. Any caller who tells you not to speak to family is running a scam.
  • Payment demands are the final step. Once you pay, the scammer exits. Call 1930 immediately after any loss.
