Key signals of AI writing
No personal incident. AI uses categories ("great food, nice people"). Humans mention something specific that happened.
Hedging phrases. "It is worth noting," "more research is needed" - fillers with no meaning.
Even rhythm. All sentences similar in length and tone. Humans are choppy and inconsistent.
Resolved endings. AI wraps up neatly. Humans trail off or contradict themselves.
