# E-Commerce & Marketplace Deception

## Sub-topics

- Dynamic pricing — being charged more based on your device, browser, or location
- Drip pricing — base price shown at the top, fees and charges added only at checkout
- Fake reference prices and manufactured discounts — the crossed-out price that was never real
- Search result manipulation — paid placement disguised as organic rankings on shopping platforms
- Counterfeit products sold through legitimate marketplace platforms
- Returns and warranty manipulation — policies designed to be difficult to use
- Flash sale psychology and manufactured scarcity on e-commerce platforms
