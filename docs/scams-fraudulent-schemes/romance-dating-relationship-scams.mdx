# Romance, Dating & Relationship Scams

## Sub-topics

- Building trust over weeks or months before the ask
- Romantic stories and personas that never add up
- Pig butchering — long-term con before requesting money
- Requests for photos or verification as leverage
- Isolation from friends and sceptics
- Recognising the scam and emotional recovery
