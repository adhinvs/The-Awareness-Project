# Your Digital Behaviour & Habits

## Sub-topics

- Think before you click — the one-second pause habit that catches most attacks
- Think before you share — personal information, location data, and life events online
- Recognising when something feels off — trusting instinct as a genuine security signal
- Who to call when something goes wrong — building your personal response plan before you need it
- Teaching digital safety to the people around you — family members, elderly relatives, and children
