# Fear, Desire & Identity-Based Selling

## Sub-topics

- Fear-based marketing — selling solutions to manufactured or exaggerated threats
- Desire-based selling — aspirational advertising and the gap it creates
- Identity marketing — selling products as expressions of who you are or want to be
- Shame and inadequacy as commercial levers — beauty, fitness, and wellness industry tactics
- Health and wellness marketing manipulation — miracle claims and pseudo-science
- Recognising when an ad is selling an emotion rather than a product
