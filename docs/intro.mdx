# Welcome to The Awareness Project

The Awareness Project is a comprehensive, research-based curriculum designed to help you see through **Digital Risk and Manipulation**. Our mission is to equip you with the psychological and technical tools needed to navigate the modern digital world safely.

## 📱 How to Navigate

Whether you are here to learn about deepfakes, financial scams, or dark patterns, all our content is organized into logical sections.

If you are viewing this on a mobile device, look for the **hamburger menu** (the three horizontal lines `☰`) at the top of your screen. Tapping it will open the sidebar where all sections and modules are sorted for easy access.
:::

## 🛠️ The 6-Step Learning Flow

Every module in this project follows a strict **6-Step Architecture** to ensure you don't just read, but actually *learn* and *apply*:

1. **[Story] The Hook**: A relatable scenario to explain the core concept.
2. **[Facts] The Grounding**: Real-world data and statistics to prove the threat is real.
3. **[Interactive] The Engagement**: A simulated component where you interact with the risk.
4. **[Unpack] The Analysis**: A breakdown of the Threat Taxonomy (Vector, Motivation, Vulnerability, Target).
5. **[Action] The Application**: A 3-to-5 step checklist of what you should do right now.
6. **[Pause] The Assessment**: A quick test to verify your knowledge retention.
